// About Me

Ever since I can remember, I've been moved by stories, whether in book, film, or personal experience, that have a central figure overcoming significant obstacles. I love the emotional connection that is naturally created from these situations. Later in life, I've learned that I have a great deal of empathy and that even further down life's path, I discovered that I am an HSP. (Highly Sensitive Person). Some would say that this could be limiting or a weakness, but I've come to learn that during my UX Journey, it is a tremendous strength. With this sensitivity, I am continually having the user journey in mind while focusing on the problem that needs to be solved.
I am now am looking to transfer my experience as a Visual Designer and my acquired skills from Denver University UX/UI Bootcamp to the field of User Experience / User Interface Design. To have these creative and technical skills isn’t enough, as I’ve learned over the years. Interfacing with your team collaboratively while taking responsibility for your workload has been an important balance that aligns the power of group brainstorming with the ability to test and grow your skills as a Designer personally.
